About Kyakapeya Wap

Kyakapeya Wap is a Ugandan software company and video game developer and publisher based in Hoima, Uganda.

The company focuses on creating digital solutions, software projects, interactive entertainment, and video game experiences for users around the world.

Founded on 11 April 2023, Kyakapeya Wap continues to explore innovation through technology, creativity, and digital media.

CEO & Founder

Asaba Jumah

Asaba Jumah is a Ugandan actor, musician, author, film producer, and CEO of Kyakapeya Wap.

He has appeared in several independent films and continues developing his career in both the film and music industries while leading technology and creative projects through Kyakapeya Wap.

He was born to Mr. Muhumuza Elias and Ms. Balikya Hadijah in Hoima, Uganda.
