About Kyakapeya Wap

Kyakapeya Wap is a Ugandan software company and video game developer and publisher based in Hoima, Uganda.

The company focuses on software development, digital innovation, interactive entertainment, and video game experiences.

Founded on 11 April 2023, Kyakapeya Wap combines creativity and technology to develop future digital products.

Founder & CEO

Asaba Jumah

Asaba Jumah is a Ugandan actor, musician, author, film producer, media producer and CEO of Kyakapeya Wap.

He leads Kyakapeya Wap while building projects across technology, entertainment, film and digital media.

Born in Hoima, Uganda, Asaba Jumah is the son of Mr. Muhumuza Elias and Ms. Balikya Hadijah.
