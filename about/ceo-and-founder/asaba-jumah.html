Asaba Jumah

Founder & CEO of Kyakapeya Wap

Asaba Jumah is a Ugandan actor, musician, author, film producer and technology entrepreneur. He is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Kyakapeya Wap, a Ugandan software company and video game developer and publisher based in Hoima, Uganda.

About Asaba Jumah

Asaba Jumah was born on 23 September 2005 in Hoima, Uganda. He developed an interest in storytelling, acting, music and technology from a young age.

He is the son of Mr. Muhumuza Elias and Ms. Balikya Hadijah.

He has built a career across film, music, publishing and digital innovation.

Founder & CEO of Kyakapeya Wap

Founded on 11 April 2023, Kyakapeya Wap is a Ugandan software company, video game developer and publisher.

As CEO, Asaba Jumah leads the company's vision in software development, digital entertainment and technology projects.
