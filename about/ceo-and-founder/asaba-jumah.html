Asaba Jumah

Founder & CEO of Kyakapeya Wap

Asaba Jumah is a Ugandan actor, musician, author, film producer, and entrepreneur from Hoima, Uganda. He is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Kyakapeya Wap, a Ugandan software company and video game developer and publisher.

About Asaba Jumah

Asaba Jumah (born 23 September 2005) is a Ugandan creative entrepreneur known for his work in acting, music, writing, and digital media.

He has appeared in several independent films and continues to develop his career in the Ugandan film and music industry.

Beyond entertainment, Asaba Jumah leads Kyakapeya Wap as Founder and CEO, where he focuses on software development, technology innovation, and video game publishing.

Early Life

Asaba Jumah was born on 23 September 2005 in Hoima, Uganda. Growing up in Uganda's cultural environment, he developed interest in storytelling, film, music, and creativity from an early age.

His passion for entertainment and technology inspired him to explore different creative fields including acting, music production, writing, and digital innovation.

CEO & Founder of Kyakapeya Wap

Asaba Jumah founded Kyakapeya Wap on 11 April 2023. The company is a Ugandan software company and video game developer and publisher based in Hoima, Uganda.

Under his leadership, Kyakapeya Wap focuses on creating digital products, software solutions, interactive entertainment, and technology projects.
